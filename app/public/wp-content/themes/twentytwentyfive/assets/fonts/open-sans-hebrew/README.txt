Place the following font files in this directory:

  OpenSansHebrew-Regular.ttf   (font-weight: 400)
  OpenSansHebrew-Bold.ttf      (font-weight: 700)

These files are referenced by @font-face declarations in assets/css/map.css.

Download Open Sans Hebrew from:
  https://fonts.google.com/specimen/Open+Sans+Hebrew
  or the original source at https://github.com/googlefonts/opensans

Until these files are present the panel will render with Arial Hebrew / Arial as fallback.
